# RoadWise 

**AI-powered app that detects distracted or unsafe driving and rewards safe driving behavior to eliminate driver distractions.**

## The Problem: Distracted Driving

Distracted driving is one of the leading causes of accidents on roadways today. According to the National Safety Council, over **6% of car accidents** and nearly **12% of fatal collisions** can be attributed to phone use while driving. This doesn't include other forms of distraction such as:

- Eating or drinking while driving
- Adjusting vehicle controls
- Talking to passengers
- Fatigue and drowsiness
- Speeding and reckless behavior

The consequences are devastating: lost lives, injuries, financial costs, and psychological trauma for those involved. RoadWise addresses this critical issue by combining modern AI technology with behavioral incentives to promote safer driving habits.

## The Solution: RoadWise

RoadWise is a mobile application that encourages safer driving by:

1. **Detecting Distracted Driving** - Monitors driver behavior and detects signs of distraction
2. **Tracking Driving Metrics** - Records comprehensive driving data including speed, location, and driving patterns
3. **Rewarding Safe Behavior** - Awards points for safe, focused drives that can be redeemed for real rewards
4. **AI-Powered Insights** - Provides personalized driver reports and safety scores to help users improve
5. **Rewards System** - Gamifies safe driving through streak tracking and achievement systems
6. **Family Safety** - Includes group tracking and emergency features for family members

## Key Features

### 📊 Dashboard & Personal Stats
- **Points System** - Earn points for every safe drive
- **Drive Counter** - Track total number of drives completed
- **Driving Streaks** - Build consecutive safe drives with visual streak indicators

- **Background Location Tracking** - Continuous location monitoring with smart distance/time filtering
